Introduction to the AML Team

Global Compliance is a risk management function overseeing regulatory compliance, product compliance, financial crimes, and enterprise risk management through oversight, advice, and controls. Global Compliance, part of the Legal Org, consists of five closely coordinated pillars. Financial Crimes (FC) is one of the pillars.

FC helps to protect Stripe and its users from financial crimes. Our work includes financial crime prevention (stopping criminals from using our platform) and financial crime compliance (adherence to regulatory and partner requirements).

FC Operations at SDC consists of four teams: Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Sanctions, Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) and Financial Crimes Ops Support (FoS).

Our Vision

To be the operational powerhouse behind Stripe's Financial Crime function, ensuring safe and successful deployment of products. We're committed to excellence, making every process airtight, efficient, and effective. As stewards of trust, we operationalize rigorous standards to keep our platform secure and users confident.

Our Strategy

Our strategy aligns us around the work we need to do together to achieve success. By outlining a clear strategy all SDC FC Stripes can understand, we distill it into a concrete, measurable set of SDC-FC-level SOKRs to evaluate our progress throughout the upcoming year.
