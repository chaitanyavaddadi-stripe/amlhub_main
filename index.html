Who We Are

Global Compliance is Stripe's risk management function, overseeing regulatory and product compliance, financial crimes, and enterprise risk. Financial Crimes (FC) is one of five closely coordinated pillars, helping protect Stripe and its users from financial crimes through prevention and compliance.

"FC Operations at SDC consists of four teams: AML, Sanctions, EDD, and FoS."

Our Vision

To be the operational powerhouse behind Stripe's Financial Crime function, ensuring safe and successful deployment of products. We're committed to excellence, making every process airtight, efficient, and effective. As stewards of trust, we operationalize rigorous standards to keep our platform secure and users confident.

The Four Pillars

Our team's foundation for excellence, innovation, and culture.

Pillar 1: Run the Business

DRI: Vinayak Shenoy K
Focus: Operational excellence, delivery, execution
  • • Managing day-to-day responsibilities
  • • Meeting SLAs, deadlines, and KPIs
  • • Ownership and accountability
  • • Proactive risk and issue management

This pillar is about keeping the lights on. It ensures we reliably deliver on our core responsibilities, maintain stakeholder trust, and stay consistent in our performance.

Pillar 2: Change the Business

DRI: Mohit Aditya
Focus: Innovation, automation, process improvement, strategic thinking
  • • Rethinking current ways of working
  • • Driving initiatives to simplify, automate, or modernize
  • • Learning from retrospectives and performance data
  • • Trying new tools, tech, or methods

This is how we future-proof the team. By continuously improving, we increase efficiency, adaptability, and the long-term impact of our work.

Pillar 3: People & Culture

DRI: Divya Nayak
Focus: Team engagement, growth, wellbeing, inclusion
  • • Fostering psychological safety and team connection
  • • Supporting individual growth plans and learning
  • • Encouraging regular feedback and recognition
  • • Advancing DEI efforts and a strong sense of belonging

A healthy, motivated team outperforms. This pillar focuses on the how behind the what—ensuring we're a high-functioning, supportive, and inclusive team.

Pillar 4: Quality & Craft

DRI: Aparna Jayant
Focus: Excellence in delivery and discipline-specific best practices
  • • Peer reviews, testing, and coding/content standards
  • • Learning from bugs, mistakes, and feedback
  • • Investing in tools and upskilling
  • • Taking pride in the craft and final outcomes

It's not just about delivery—it's about delivering valuable, sustainable, high-quality work we're proud of.
